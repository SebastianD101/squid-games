How to play:
You have 5 marbles, your opponent has 5 marbles
You have to guess whether your opponent is thinking of an even or odd number
Guess it right? You receive your bet from your opponent
Guess it wrong? You give your bet to your opponent
If you get to 10 marbles, you may continue to the next round
If you get to 0 marbles, you will be eliminated
